About me

Currently I am an assistant researcher at Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences and pusuing my Ph.D. degree under the supvision of Prof. Enhua Wu. My research interests are mainly focused on computer graphics, computational physics, and computational fabrication, with especial focus on computational fluid dynamics. My thesis research was to develop numerical algorithms on simplicial complexes that can model various complex fluid phenomena with different types of highly codimensinal structures.
