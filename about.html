About Me

I am a research associate professor at the Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences. My research interests are mainly focused on computer graphics, computational physics, smoothed particle hydrodynamics, peridynamics and nolocal theory. My purpose is to develop accurate, efficient and robust algorithms that can model various complex materials with different types of codimensinal structures.
